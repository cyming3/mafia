The Origin of M.A.F.I.A

"M.A.F.I.A" is actually a game concept that inspired from "detective-puzzle" games as well as some variety shows. "M.A.F.I.A" game was first introduced by me myself to my church fellowship, back in late 2017. Rebranded with logo and applying posters to new themes since 2021.

Driven by a background story setting, players are given characters and various identity that disclose from other players, whether you are the "civillian" (good guy) and "imposter" (bad guy).

To complete the game, players not only have to solves the puzzles, but they also required to obtained clues or hints or special props through winning mini-games. This not only enrich the game from pure investigating, but boost up the excitement, team-building, as well as training observation and problem solving.

Finally, this is just a mock website for assignment. Although this games are real and played before at my church fellowship, but it definitely not distributed in real-life. Thank you, and I am Yong Ming, the creator of "M.A.F.I.A" games.
